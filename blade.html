Balloon Launch Assessment Directive for Everyone

The Balloon Launch Assessment Directive for Everyone, or BLADE, is the quickest way for students to gain the most experience.
Groups of students work together to design a mission for a 1-U satellite

I was a team leader in the BLADE program. I led a team of 7 multidisciplinary first year students to design a project. Our project name was "Luminosity"

The project's mission was to collect and measure light pollution on the ground. We went through a long process with multiple presentations including CoDR, PDR, CDR, and other presentations that were all shown to industry engineers. This is probably one of my favorite projects to talk about because it fell slightly short. Despite this, it was one of my greatest learning experiences that inspired me to keep working hard. In doing so, for the academic year of 2023-2024, I became a part of Bronco Space's Executive Board as Engineering Coordinator.

The avionics were ran using a raspberry pi, heated using kapton heaters, and powered by 18650 Lithium ion batteries.
