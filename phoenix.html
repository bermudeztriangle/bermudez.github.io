Project Phoenix

The Rocket Propelled Lander Project, or Project Phoenix, is
Cal Poly Pomona's self-landing rocket program with Bronco Space

Who is Bronco Space? Bronco Space is Cal Poly Pomona's "official" space program. It is a club consisting of multiple projects, including this one, that all expose students to hands-on learning and industry processes.

I am currently a Student Guidance, Navigation, and Controls Engineer. My work consisted of MATLAB coding and Simulink to simulate the behavior of the rocket's thrust vector control system as it moves through the air.

This is a picture of what our lander looks like in Solidworks Fusion360

To give our team members something to visualize and to make sure that we fully understand the concept of a thrust vector controlled system, the team and I created an inverted pendulum as a stepping stone to different versions
