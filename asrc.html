ASRC

During the summer of 2023, I had the wonderful opportunity to be a Mission Integration intern at Vandenberg Space Force Base
for the Arctic Slopes Regional Corporation.

For 10 weeks, I worked under two direct report squadrons under Space Launch Delta 30 (SLD 30)/Vandenberg SFB

The 2nd Range Operations Squadron (2ROPS) and the 2nd Space Launch Squadron (2SLS)

For the first five weeks of my internship, I worked with 2ROPS, a range squadron dedicated to ensuring range safety. My job had me involved in server rooms and working with server racks while also learning more about the large world of contracting.

For the latter weeks of my internship, I moved over to 2SLS. Their mission is to provide mission assurance and integration to the base's customers.
