My name is Adrian Bermudez and I grew up in the town of Palmdale, California. I am studying aerospace engineering at the California State Polytechnic University, Pomona.

I am a curious and outgoing person who loves spending time with friends and family. As for hobbies, I have plenty (with many more to come)! These hobbies include: basketball, football, playing music (drums, bass, guitar), video games, paintballing, shooting photos, and messing around with RC aircraft and cars. I have built a few RC aircraft in the past and is a part of the reason why I went into the field of aerospace.
