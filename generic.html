My name is Adrian Bermudez and I grew up in the town of Palmdale, California. I am studying aerospace engineering at the California State Polytechnic University, Pomona.

I am a curious and outgoing person who loves spending time with friends and family. As for hobbies, I have plenty! These hobbies include: basketball, football, playing music (drums, guitar, bass), video games, shooting photos, paintballing, and messing around with RC aircraft and cars. I have built a few RC aircraft in the past and is a part of the reason of why I went into the field of aerospace. I am also a Star Wars guy (if you couldn't tell by the picture) and am open to learning and trying new things. I hope to become my family's first aerospace engineer and become the best person that I can be.

As mentioned, I like to take photos. The photo (top) is me and my brother taking pictures at Palmdale's Blackbird Air Park. The lower picture (bottom) is a picture that I took of the Thunderbirds at the Edwards Air Force Base Air Show. The other photos are of me and a couple of my favorite hobbies.
